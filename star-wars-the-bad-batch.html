About

Star Wars: The Bad Batch is an American animated television series created by Dave Filoni for the streaming service Disney+. It is part of the Star Wars franchise, serving as a sequel to Star Wars: The Clone Wars (2008-2020). The series is produced by Lucasfilm Animation, with Jennifer Corbett as head writer and Brad Rau as supervising director. It was officially ordered by Disney+ in July 2020 as a spin-off from The Clone Wars' final season, with Filoni, Corbett, and Rau attached.
